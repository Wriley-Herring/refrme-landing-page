How RefrMe Works

Follow these simple steps to grow your business through effective referrals.

Set Your Referral Incentive

Offer an attractive incentive, like 20% off your next service, for every successful referral.

Share Your Unique Link or QR Code

Easily share your unique link or QR code with your customers.

Customers See the Incentive

Customers view the incentive and submit their referral information.

Receive a Notification

Get notified instantly when a new referral is added.

Track Referrals

Monitor who referred whom and track the progress of each referral.

Convert Referrals

Manage referrals through to conversion, and reward customers with their incentive upon success.
